about me

Greetings! I'm Arian Sergi, and I hope you've enjoyed exploring my portfolio thus far. Armed with a Master's degree in Advanced Computer Science with Advanced Practice, my programming journey has led me through a diverse array of development domains. My master's project delved into the realms of data science and machine learning, culminating in a significant contribution to the field with a PhD thesis at Northumbria University. I've honed my skills in various technologies, crafting projects with React, Redux, and Bootstrap for frontend development, while leveraging Node.js, ASP.NET Core, and SQL databases for backend infrastructure. Notably, I spearheaded the development of my company's inaugural commercial application independently. Presently, I'm embarking on my third application adventure, employing ASP.NET as the backend service and delving deeper into the expansive features of cloud infrastructure, particularly AWS, and its deployment options. Should you have any inquiries, please don't hesitate to reach out via the contact page.
